Without QueueMaster

Manual coordination gets harder as volume grows.

  • People ask around to find out who is next
  • Workflows depend on memory and informal updates
  • Confusion increases when the team gets busy
With QueueMaster

A visible queue helps teams stay organized.

  • Everyone sees the same up-to-date order
  • The queue advances clearly after assignments
  • The workflow feels fairer, cleaner, and easier to trust
